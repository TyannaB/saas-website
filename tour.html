What is Apple Music

Apple Music allows you to stream our catalogue of 45 million songs, along with everything in your iTunes library — no matter where it came from.Whether streaming your favourite song, album, mix, playlist or music video, you’ll never be interrupted by ads.Play every song. Even offline.

If you don’t want to use your cellular data, you can download anything from Apple Music and listen to it offline. No signal required.Discover more music. More often.Apple Music brings you up-and-coming artists and curated playlists every day.

Or try one of ours.Be your own DJ by easily making a playlist of your favourite songs or music videos. Or let Apple Music create mixes for you based on the music you like the most.
